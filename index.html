Tactical 30-Day Implementation Roadmap

With high-conversion landing pages already developed, we move immediately into technical verification and campaign deployment. This roadmap prioritizes protecting our brand first, then aggressively scaling category and competitor-based bidding.

Days 1-7: Audit & Verify

Technical Foundation

Verification of Google Tag Manager for full conversion attribution. Audit of existing landing pages to ensure "Ad-to-Page" relevance scores are maximized for Tier 2 keywords.

Days 8-15: Brand Advocacy

Defensive Activation

Deployment of Tier 1 defensive campaigns. Mapping high-intent category keywords to audited assets to establish a baseline Impression Share of >95% on brand terms.

Days 16-22: Market Penetration

Competitor Conquesting

Activation of Tier 3 aggressive bidding. Implementation of comparative messaging to intercept competitor traffic, utilizing existing specialized landing pages for direct poaching.

Days 23-30: Refinement

Analytical Optimization

Comprehensive review of Auction Insights. Real-time bid adjustments based on competitor reaction. Scaling budget toward top-performing "Competitor-to-EcoOnline" search terms.
